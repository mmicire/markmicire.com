Mark J Micire

Profile

Mark is a communicator, leader, consummate builder, and lifelong hacker with 20+ years of experience in computer science, networking, information security, human-computer interface, robotics, artificial intelligence, and mission-critical systems across commercial, space, defence, and energy sectors. Proven ability to rapidly architect and deliver scalable solutions that align emerging technology with strategic outcomes. Deep experience in industry engagement and digital transformation, including cloud robotics, distributed autonomy, and automation in extreme environments. He has been active in the cybersecurity community for over 35 years. For 25 years, he demonstrated his passion for developing solutions for social challenges through his commitment as a first responder and technical rescue specialist.
